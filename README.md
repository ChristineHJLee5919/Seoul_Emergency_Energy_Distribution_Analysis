# Emergency Energy Distribution Analysis

Simulation-based emergency energy allocation using Seoul Energy Simulation Data.

## Results

* Simulated emergency scenarios with 70%, 50%, and 30% available energy supply.
* Critical facilities such as hospitals, fire/police services, and communication infrastructure maintained high allocation rates.
* Commercial and industrial facilities experienced the largest shortages under severe energy constraints.
* Demonstrated the effectiveness of a priority-based energy allocation strategy for improving energy resilience.
